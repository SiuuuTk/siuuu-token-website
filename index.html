What is SIUUU?

SIUUU Token is a community-driven cryptocurrency inspired by the energy, excellence, and iconic celebrations of Cristiano Ronaldo. Built on the BSC (Binance Smart Chain), SIUUU rewards holders with deflationary mechanics and community involvement.

How Does SIUUU Work?

Deflationary Mechanism

SIUUU Token employs a deflationary model where a small percentage of each transaction is burned, reducing the overall supply. This leads to scarcity and potential appreciation over time as the supply decreases.

Rewards for Holders

A percentage of each transaction is redistributed to holders. This means that the more you hold, the more rewards you receive! This system encourages long-term holding and supports the growth of the community.

Transaction Fees

  • Burn Fee: A percentage of each transaction is permanently burned, decreasing the supply.
  • Reward Fee: A percentage is distributed to existing holders to incentivize keeping the token.
